Why Another Water Filter Company?

Quality drinking water is an oft-misunderstood concept. When it exists, it is unappreciated, and when it doesn’t, you panic. But in the middle of this spectrum is the fact that children and those with immune deficiency often get very sick from drinking contaminated water. At Nature’s Way we believe that “safe” healthy drinking water should be easily accessible.

Boiling Water

Boiling Water is not always effective for the inactivation of “bacteria, viruses and protozoa”. Boiling water does not remove other harmful substances from the water; such as Arsenic, Lead, Manganese, Fluoride, Pesticides and Chemicals.

Bottled Water

Wealthier populations may use bottled water as their source of liquids. While many bottling companies are large and reputable, there is uncertainty to whether all of them conform to producing Healthy drinking water. Generally bottled water is expensive compared to alternative sources. The bottles often contain unwanted microplastics and are a serious International Environmental problem.

Reverse Osmosis

In the past Reverse osmosis (RO) point-of-use systems seemed to be a solution for the water drinking problem. It works by pushing water through a membrane that removes salts and other contaminants, leaving fairly pure water.

However, this process removes vital mineral content necessary to maintain the Human Health and causes the water to become highly aggressive, easily dissolving materials it comes into contact with. Treatment plants would remineralise the water to stop it from leaching piping materials back into the water. But this also has effects on health.

The World Health Organisation have found that higher levels of calcium, and magnesium have correlated with lower levels of cardiovascular disease. Demineralised water was found to also leach electrolytes from tissues and cause a whole range of health problems. While the WHO focuses on the role of calcium and magnesium, other important minerals are stripped away such as fluorides that have beneficial effects on dental health. The interesting thing was that effects such as "increased cardiovascular disorders, tiredness, weakness [and] muscular cramps” were reported only after a few months. While diet could potentially provide the required nutrients, the modern diet is often deficient of the right amount of nutrients. Micronutrients found in water are also in free ion form, which are better absorbed than if it were consumed as food. Some RO POU purifiers add a calcium remineralization cartridge at the end-point, often this is only to reduce the aggressive nature of the water. Other essential minerals are not re-added.

RO membranes also faces a structural problem in that their membranes are susceptible to bacteria growth (biofouling) and prone to leakages. This requires that they need to replaced very regularly to briefly re-establish their stated water quality.
